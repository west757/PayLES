Common LES Remarks

If TSP election amount exceeds net amount due, TSP will not be deducted.
  - The contribution limit for the TSP is $23,500 per year. Once the limit is reached, no additional TSP deductions will occur.
You are the best manager of your pay. Preclude overpayment or underpayment of your pay by reviewing your LES every month. Visit your local finance office for any updates required.
  - Army: S1 Shop
  - Air Force: Customer Support Section (CSS)

Make sure to use your benefit: you can save an average of 25% on your grocery bill by shopping at the Commisary, in-store and online at Commissary Click2Go! Visit corp.commissaries.com.
If eligible, you may have been automatically enrolled in the airborne hazards and open burn pit registry. Visit health.mil/ahburnpitregistry to learn more.
Active Duty families: explore your FEDVIP vision benefits at the virtual benefits fair. Visit benefeds.com/military.
Health care flexible spending accounts allow eligible service members to set aside up to $3,300 in pre-tax earnings for health care costs. Enroll at fsafeds.gov/enroll. Learn more at finred.usalearning.gov/hcfsa.
The Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA) protects service members. These protections include reducing interest rates and permitting termination of a lease upon PCS/Deployment. Visit aflegalassistance.law.af.mil.
Receive your 1095 and W2 statements faster and more secure by logging onto MyPay at mypay.dfas.mil and selecting the turn on/off hardcopy 1095 and W2 option to elect electronic only.

Start accession.
  - Provides the date in which the member was fully provisioned into the service.
